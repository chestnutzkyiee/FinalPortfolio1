What is Type4 Technology Group?

highest possible level we can achieve is the Type 4 civilization.

in technology. It is founded on January 27, 2023, where the
current headquarter is located in Philippines.

This company is ready to aim the Type 4 Civilization advancement.
With the help of the existing technologies, the company utilize
its ability as much as possible to upgrade in the next level
of civilization.
